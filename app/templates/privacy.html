Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our travel itinerary platform ("WIMIA", "we", "us", or "our").

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Full name, email address, password (hashed).
  • Travel Details: Trip names, destinations, dates, flight info, hotel/accommodation info, and assignments.
  • Device Information: Browser type, IP address, and general location for analytics and security.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To display your personalized travel itineraries.
  • To send you important notifications (like schedule updates or assignments).
  • To improve and secure the app.

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal data. We only share your information in the following cases:

  • With authorized team administrators for itinerary management purposes.
  • With trusted services like email providers (e.g., Gmail API) solely to send itinerary emails.
  • If required by law (e.g., court order, subpoena).

4. Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies or browser storage to remember your login session and improve app performance. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures including encryption and secure storage. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You may request to view, update, or delete your personal data by contacting us. If you are part of an organization, your access may depend on their policies.

7. Third-Party Links

This app may link to external services (e.g., Airbnb, airline sites). We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Review their policies before submitting any information.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new "Effective Date."

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, contact us at admin@wimia.com.
